Welcome to RESU - Pinoy Community

A united Filipino gaming community built on teamwork, fun, and respect.

About Us

RESU stands for Resident Evil Survival Unit — a Pinoy community dedicated to fans of Resident Evil and survival-horror games. We are a hub for Filipino gamers who enjoy survival, horror, and cooperative adventures. Our goal is to create a fun, safe, and respectful space where members can enjoy events, share knowledge, and build lasting friendships through gaming.

Who We Are

We are passionate Filipino fans of the Resident Evil franchise and survival-horror games. Our community is made up of casual players, competitive gamers, and long-time enthusiasts who all share the same goal: surviving together and having fun while doing it.

What Makes Us Interesting

Our community isn’t just about gaming — it’s about connection. By joining RESU, you’ll have access to:

  • Exclusive survival challenges and themed events.
  • Friendly teammates ready to play and strategize with you.
  • A respectful environment that values teamwork and sportsmanship.
  • Discussions about Resident Evil lore, tips, and fan creations.

Our Mission

We believe gaming is best enjoyed together. Our mission is to create a welcoming community where Filipino gamers can explore survival challenges, celebrate Resident Evil, and most importantly — have fun while building friendships that last beyond the game.
